Technology

Turmgel is an innovative product that combines the the core principles of Ayurveda, with a modern drug delivery system, in the form of mouth-dissolving lozenges. This product is the outcome of intense patented research and has been specifically developed for the slow release of Curcumin into the buccal cavity of the mouth. This form of delivery, makes Turmgel easy to consume, easy to carry, and suitable for all ages.

  • 100% rapid absorption of the bioactive form through the buccal cavity and direct entry into body's blood circulation system.
  • Buccal absorption bypasses the hostile environment of the gastrointestinal tract.

Difference Between Buccal Absorption and
Conventional Oral Administration (Capsules/ Tablets)

BUCCAL ABSORPTION

  • Quick, as absorption begins in the oral cavity.
  • Bypasses the hostile environment of the gastrointestinal tract.
  • Rapid absorption and direct entry into the systemic circulation makes the treatment effective and almost instantaneous.
  • Can be taken anywhere and used without water.
  • Safe for both adults and children.
  • Ideal for those suffering from digestive complaints that can hinder the absorption of nutrients.

CONVENTIONAL ORAL ABSORPTION

  • Longer transit time through the gastrointestinal tract, influenced by gastric emptying time, presence of food etc.).
  • Hostile gastrointestinal environment leads to degradation of the product.
  • First pass effect and bio-transformation processes determine the drug bioavailability.
  • Needs water to swallow which could be problematic for elderly patients and is unsuitable for young children.
